Human mission to Mars

A human mission to Mars has been the subject of science fiction since the 1880, and aerospace engineering and scientific proposals since the late 1940. Plans include landing on Mars for exploration at a minimum, with some considering exploring its Phobos and Deimos. Longterm proposals have included sending settlers and starting terraforming of the planet
